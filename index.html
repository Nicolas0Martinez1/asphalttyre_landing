About

What is Asphalt tyre?

Asphalt Tyre is the first vehicle metaverse based on Play to Earn on the solana blockchain, where players will obtain NFTs of motorcycles, cars, mechanic workshops, race tracks, among other things, in order to compete and be the best.

Economy

Our economy is going to be subject to the solana (Sol) token because we believe that, being a valuable currency, with foundations and bases within its own blockchain, it generates a benefit for the user with fast and fluid transactions. It also has very low costs, and generates lasting economic profitability over time.

Investors and players

Asphalt Tyre is being built, thought and developed so that players and investors can be part of this world, either by playing, owning the various NFTs of vehicles, racing circuits, mechanical workshops, owning a racing team, getting advertising or being a sponsor and betting on the races.
