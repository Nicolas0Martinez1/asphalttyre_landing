How to play

To play it is necessary to have an NFT vehicle to be able to access certain tracks and be able to compete.
Each vehicle belongs to a specific category, therefore it can only compete in the circuits that its corresponding category allows.
There is also the possibility of renting an NFT vehicle from a "Fleet" to participate.

Mechanical workshop

The players or the "Fleets" must enter their vehicles inside it to obtain improvements.
There will be a limited number of mechanical workshops in NFT format, within them, when buying the workshop, they will be granted a limited number of components for improvements.
Workshop owners will need to purchase upgrade NFTs and resell them at a stipulated price range.
Players or "Fleets" will have their vehicles upgraded and will be able to compete for greater prizes and rewards.

Investment

The game can be accessed not only as a player, but also as an investor.
Through the purchase of NFT circuits, you will have the power of the purchased circuit and will receive economic rewards for each tournament that is played in it, as well as percentages of advertisements that appear in your circuit.
They could also create a "Fleet" in which to access to own one it is necessary to have at least 5 NFT of the vehicles to form them.

Analystics

We plan to have our own tokenomics in the future, for usability within our ecosystem with various functionalities.
Our vision is clear, to create the game so that players have the opportunity to have fun playing, while earning money as well as creating a sustainable economy within it.
