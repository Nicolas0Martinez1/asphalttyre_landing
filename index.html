FAQS

What is Asphalt tyre?

Asphalt Tyre is the first Play to Earn-based vehicle metaverse on the Solana blockchain, where players will earn NFTs of motorcycles, cars, garages, racetracks, among other things, in order to compete and be the best.
Because it is hosted on the solana network? Because we believe that the Solana Blockchain has great potential and scalability, as well as fast transactions and low gas costs.

Economy

Our economy is going to be subject to the solana token (Sol) since we believe that being a currency of value, with fundamentals and bases within its own blockchain, it generates a benefit for the user with fast and fluid transactions, as well as costs very low, and also generate lasting economic profitability over time.

Investors and players

Asphalt Tyre is being built, thought and developed so that players and investors can be part of this world, whether playing, owning the various Nfts of vehicles, racing circuits, mechanical workshops, owning a scuderia, getting publicity or being sponsor and through betting on the races.

How to have a Scuderia?

To be part of a team and its benefits, it is necessary to have at least 5 NFTs of the same category, that is, if I want to form a "Legendary" class team, I need 5 (five) NFTs that have these characteristics.

How to enter Asphalt Tyre?

To enter to be part, it is necessary to have at least one NFTs of any vehicle and class, or I can enter if a team hires me to be players on their team.
